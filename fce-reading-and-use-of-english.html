FCE Reading & Use of English

The Reading & Use of English paper is an important part of the Cambridge B2 First (FCE) exam. It tests your vocabulary, grammar, and reading skills.

What’s in this paper?

  • Part 1: Multiple-choice cloze
  • Part 2: Open cloze
  • Part 3: Word formation
  • Part 4: Key word transformations
  • Part 5: Multiple choice reading
  • Part 6: Gapped text
  • Part 7: Multiple matching
